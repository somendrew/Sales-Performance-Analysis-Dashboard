# Sales Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance data, focusing on key metrics such as total sales, profits, and quantity for the current year and the previous year.

## Key Requirements
- Display a summary of total sales, profits, and quantity for the current year and the previous year.
- Present monthly sales trends for both years, highlighting months with highest and lowest sales.
- Compare sales performance by product subcategory for both years, including sales and profit comparison.
- Analyze weekly sales and profit trends for the current year, highlighting weeks above and below average.

## Features
- **KPI Overview**: Summary of total sales, profits, and quantity for CY and PY.
- **Sales Trends**: Monthly sales data for CY and PY, with highlights on highest and lowest sales months.
- **Product Subcategory Comparison**: Sales and profit comparison by product subcategory for CY and PY.
- **Weekly Trends**: Weekly sales and profit data for CY, with average values and highlights on above/below average weeks.

## Data Requirements
- Sales data for current year and previous year
- Product subcategory data
- Date-wise sales and profit data
